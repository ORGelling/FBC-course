Exercise 3: Perform file operations without using iostream

We use copy_file() and last_write_time to duplicate our file and set the 
timestamp to the original one. We run the program as such:

$ tmp/bin/binary main.cc main.copy

and see:

$ ls -l main.*
-rw-rw-r-- 1 olivier olivier 279 Nov 15 22:46 main.cc
-rw-rw-r-- 1 olivier olivier 279 Nov 15 22:46 main.copy
-rw-rw-r-- 1 olivier olivier 236 Nov 15 22:47 main.ih
