Exercise 43: design a scanner scanning a piece of text

We note that the exercise asks us to hand in our lexer(.txt) and our scanner.h
file. It also goes into depth aobut how to properly amend a file while reading
it (using a temporary file and making sure its .tmp extended name does not
conflict with others), but that seems, not just, as the exercise mentions,
outside the scope of the purpose of the exercise, but also outside the scope
of the two files we are asked to hand in. The class that manages the file
stream would more likely be part of the program that *uses* the scanner, and
should not be part of the scanner itself.
