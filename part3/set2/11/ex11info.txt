To do this we inherit a base class that has the static s_count and s_actual
members in a protected section, and manipulate its components inside the con-
and destructors. The Unique class can be simplified now, as the functionality
of the Counter base is entirely relegated to its constructor and destructor. 

We have left both a total and actual count in this global counter, as the
exercise does imply that we should keep track of both of these, even for the
global counting mechanics. We have, however, moved the data members to the
private section, as the manipulation thereof happens by calling the con- and
de-structors. This encapsulates the functionality of this Counter class
exactly as it should. We place the c- and dtor in the protected section
instead, to make sure they can only be called as a sub-object of derived 
classes.
